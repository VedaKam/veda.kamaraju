Cornell DEBUT

I helped design a cane with a built-in tuned-mass damper to reduce hand tremors. We CAD-modeled a TPU “bosu” base with traction treads and ran **IRB-approved** tests with Parkinson’s patients, collecting **80,000+** accelerometer samples and evaluating peak reduction vs. each patient’s baseline tremor.

Tools: CAD, TPU 3D printing, MATLAB, human factors & biomechanics.

Research

De Vlaminck Lab: spatial/epigenetic patterns in liver carcinoma. Sabuncu Lab: bridging **clinical workflow** gaps; ordinal models for Alzheimer’s prognosis. Wyss Institute: GNN pipeline + BioNeMo experiments; presented at **2025 CMBE**.

Stack: Python/R, Scanpy/AnnData, GNNs, BioNeMo.
