About Me

My name is Veda Kam, and I’m a third-year student at Cornell University, originally from the Bay Area, California. I study Information Science, Systems, and Technology in the College of Engineering. My primary focus is to create solutions at the intersections of healthcare and technology.

I am currently exploring AI-driven algorithms in pathology for early diagnosis, biomechanical product development for Parkinson's patients, and computational design for drug delivery applications. I strive to integrate various disciplines to bridge real-world gaps and craft innovative solutions.

At Cornell, I’m the co-Team Lead of the premier biomedical engineering project team, DEBUT, the president of the South Asian music group, Saarang, and the VP of Partnerships at the entrepreneurship accelerator, LCL.

Future Ambitions

I plan on pursuing a grad program in BME or Computational Biology, leveraging my experiences in these past experiences to contribute to the real-world. I hope to continue my two pronged appraoch with an integration of computational sciences and biomechanical engineering. I'm interested in anything entrepreneurship and health tech related and always looking for opportunites to connect!
